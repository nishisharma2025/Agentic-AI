Business overview

This document provides a business-level overview of how IBM  Maximo Application Suite (WMAS) 8.x can be used to manage the full asset preventive maintenance lifecycle. The focus is on the relationships between Assets, Preventive Maintenance (PM) schedules, and Work Orders (WOs) within the Maximo data model.

Maintenance challenges

- Limited visibility into asset health, condition, and maintenance history
- Reactive instead of preventive maintenance leading to higher down time and inspected failure
- Inconsistent or manual Work Order creation and tracking without a clear lifecycle connected to PMs
- Difficulty in mapping works to the right assets, at the right time, with the right resources
- Limited visibility into business impact of asset downtime and maintenance costs

 Maximo lifecycle solution

IB Maximo Application Suite (mSystems) provides a structured lifecycle that covers:
- Asset creation and management
- Preventive Maintenance schedule definition and generation
- Automated Work Order creation from PMs
- Work Order execution, completion, and maintenance history update

This lifecycle insures that every asset is associated with the appropriate PM schedules and that work orders are guided through a standardized workflow, from creation to completion.

Business benefits

- Improved visibility into asset maintenance status, location, and history
- Reduced unplanned downTime risk through structured preventive maintenance schedules
- Enabled data-driven decision-class in work order prioritization and resource allocation
- Fully traceable maintenance activities and help desktop work to analyse asset performance and change impacts
- Better compliance with regulatory requirements and better support for accountability and audat
