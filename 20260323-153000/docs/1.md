# Business Use Case - Maximo PM (Safety Inspection based) Work Order Automation

## 1. Overview

This document describes an automated work order creation solution in Maximo Application Suite (MAS 8.x), driven by safety inspection results from preventive maintenance (PM) programs. The solution automatically creates one work order per safety inspection result when any response = FAIL.

## 2. Business Context

- Organisation: Asset-heavy company with critical, major, and minor safety inspections on work equipment, facilities, and infrastructure.
- Maintenance strategy: Preventive maintenance through Maximo Planned Maintenance (PM) tasks and Safety Inspection templates.
- Business driver: Audit, O-Maintainance, HSE, Operations Managers.

## 3. Proble Statement

Mechanically creating and management of work orders from PM-driven safety inspections is manual and error-prone. Safety issues discovered during inspections are not always recorded structured-in Maximo, and consequent prioritization of critical vs minor findings is inconsistent.

## 4. Business Objectives

1. Reduce manual data entry in work order creation for SHE,GXP, and safety-related failures by leveraging process automation (EOMP) from PM inspection results.
2. Ensure consistent, rule-based creation of one work order per individual safety inspection result when any response= FAIL.
3. Auto-assign work order priorities based on safety inspection crticality (Critical = 1, Major = 2, Minor = 3).
4. Provide visibility to stakeholders and management by persistently linking inspection results, work orders, and history in Maximo.
5. Maximize configuration for the specific organization's PM and Safety Inspection templates, while maintaining flexibility to map to additional clients and external services.
