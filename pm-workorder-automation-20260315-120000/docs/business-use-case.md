# Business Use Case: PM Work Order Automation

This document describes a business use-case for automating PM -> WO lifecycles in IBM  Maximo Application Suite 8.x.

## 1. Business Overview

- Industry: asset-intensive operations (plants/fleet/facilities).
- Goal: reduce manual WO creation, ensure ISO compliance, and improve AOR/inspection.
- Scope: preventive/paca maintenance relying on PM schedules.

## 2. Maintenance Challenges 

- Manual PM triggering and duplicated WO creation.
- Inconsistent asset association and missing data.
- Limited visibility into WM criticals and histories.
- Slow forecast response to created WOs and schedules.

## 3. End-to-end Lifecycle Solution

- Asset definition>>PM creation>>PMRGENS>WO generation>>Technician execution>>Closure>>Maintenance history update.
- Automated controls and scripts to ensure correct asset/PM/WOs associations.
- Dashboards and workflows with exception handling and exception approval.

## 4. KPIs & Metrics

- _%WEduction in hand-created PMWOs
- %Reduction in missing asset on PM WOs
- U%Av improved WO completion rates
- \%Decrease in unplanned downtime/failures
- %\Increased uniform reporting of PM and WO data

## 5. Operational Benefits

- Automated and consistent WO creation from PMs.
- Higher system usage and standardised processes.
- Improved visibility of asset status, WO status, and maintenance history.
- Foundation for future predictive maintenance capabilities.
