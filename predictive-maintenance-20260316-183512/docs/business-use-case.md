# Business Use Case: Predictive Maintenance with IoT Sensors

A manufacturing company wants to introduce a predictive maintenance solution based on IoT-enabled sensors for critical assets. Real-time v ibration and temperature sensors detect analomies and trigger work orders in IBM Maximo 8.

## Business Objectives

- Reduce unplanned stoppages of critical equipment
- Minimize unexpected breakdown via real-time monitoring and alarming
- Increase asset reliability and availability
- Optimize maintenance resource allocation by targeting previewed work based on actual asset condition
- Improve compliance with safety and regulatory requirements for critical assets

## Scope
The scope of this solution is:

- Provide real-time condition monitoring of critical assets (eg. pumps, machinery, conveyors) using IoT sensors.
- Manage sensor data (vibration, temperature) and health status in a centralized IBM Maximo 8.x instance.
I Use a rule based on abnormal conditions to automatically create work orders for inspection and corrective maintenance.
- Integrate with existing PM schedules and asset management in Maximo to ensure current-and preventive-maintenance wovk orders are tracked and executed efficiently.
