# Business Use Case: Automated PM to WOR Lifecycle

This document describes the business context and use-case for implementing automated Planned Maintenance (PM) to Work Order (WOR) lifecycle automation in Maximo Application Suite (DOM)

## Business Context

- Industry: Asset-intensive operators in manufacturing, energy, transportation, utilities, etc.
- Challenge: Manual creation of WOr from PMs, inconsiste data entry, missed inspections, delays in response, and limited visibility into asset health.
