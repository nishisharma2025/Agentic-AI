# Business Overview
Today's companies are facing increased regulatory, compliance, and safety requirements for inspection-driven operations. Manual routines, paper-based forms, and lack of real-time visibility lead to missed inspections, non-compliance, and repeated overdues.

Maximo Application Suite (MAS) 8.x enables organizations to standardize and automate inspections across equipment, facilities, and field activities, ensuring consistent coverage, compliance, and traceability.

# Maintenance Challenges
1. *Manual, Paper-Based Inspections:* Inspections are often performed using paper forms and spreadsheets, leading to inconsistent data and low traceability.
2. *Limited Visibility and Traceability:* Management has limited onsite visibility, making it difficult to assess the true status of inspections and rectify issues rapidly.
3. *Regulatory Compliance:* Different teams, stds, and numerous audit frameworks require digited and auditable track of inspection activity.
4. *Evidence ManagementFor:* Inspection records are critical for demonstrating compliance to regulatory and corporate standards.
5. *Data Integrity and Analytics:* Data is spread across multiple systems, making analysis and reporting complex.
