# Business Overview

An energy utility company with a large installed base of critical assets (motors, pumps, conveyors) is striving to reduce unplanned outage and maintenance costs. They are implementing IBM Maximo Application Suite (MAS) 8.x for centralized asset management and predictive maintenance.

## Maintenance Challenges (AsIs)

- High unplanned outage and unscheduled down time.
- Reactive maintenance practices variable by plant, article, and location.
- Limited visibility into asset health and maintenance past.- Inefficient coordination between preventive maintenance schedules, commissining, work orders, and actual performance.
- Manual, paper-based processes for planners, supervisors, and technicians.
