# Business Use Case - Breakdown Maintenance Automation

This document describes a Maximo Application Suite (MAS8 x) solution for organizations experiencing frequent asset failures. The focus is on detecting and automating breakdown maintenance patterns, and enforcing strong relationships between Asset — Maintenance Management (BMM).

##"usiness Objectives

- Riduce unplanned downtime by proactively identifying assets with risky of frequent breakdown failures
- Improve MTBF, Oee, and line availability by add-hoc filtering and prioritized response
- Enable data-driven decisions on repair/vs run-to-failure maintenance
- Enable customer-backed service levels with service agreements based on actual asset parformance
- Provide visibility into asset — Work Order relationships to support capital planning


## Business Problem Statement

Assets such as critical rotating, pumps, and crushing equipment experience repeated breakdown failures within short periods. These unexpected failures result in unplanned outage, increased maintenance costs, and pooter safety risks.

The current process is reactive and manual, lackedata driven insight into root causes, and limited rigts-validate across Asset, Maintenance task and Work Order links.

This solution seeks to automate breakdown maintenance identification by analyzing MAS data to find recurrent patterns and structurally related Asset— Work Orders.

## Scope and Boundaries

- Full views of Asset — WOR data to improve maintenance strategy and resource allocation
- Act on on early-warning signals of failure and emergent broken assets, before final failure
- Identify child assets where preventive maintenance shall be adjusted to reduce brokedown work
- Reduce MTFBs and unplanned downtime, while ensuring compliance with regulatory and safety requirements
- Enable data-driven management ready for Predictive analytics and root cause analysis

- Provide analyses suspended by asset class, model and location to help determine root causes and improve their prevention

## Stakeholders

- Asset Management Leaders
- Maintenance Engineering and Planning
- Reliability, HSE and Safety Leaders
- Operations Management, Plant Managers
- IT Enterprise Architects

## Key Business Benefits

- 30-50% reduction in breakdown MTBF over 1-2- years through better planning and decision-support
- Reduced customer impact from major change events and unplanned outage
- Improved capacity utilization and resource use by redirecting work from fire-fighting to preventive, scheduled work.
